What does direct sharing look like?

Every month, members of Samaritan Ministries give to other members with medical needs. Members pray and send their monthly share, along with notes of encouragement, directly to the member in need. This allows members to minister to all aspects of the need: spiritual, emotional, physical, and financial.

The Grimm Family

When the Grimm family received their monthly share assignment, they saw they would be sharing with the Johnsons for a maternity need. Having recently gone through a maternity need themselves, the Grimm family knew exactly how to pray for and bless the newborn and her family.

The Johnson Family

With a new baby at home, the Johnson family was encouraged by the prayer and Scriptures that came with the financial support for their maternity need. They were especially blessed by the notes and prayers coming from member families who could relate to having a newborn.
