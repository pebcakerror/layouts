It’s kind of like sharing juice with friends. Maybe there’s enough for four friends to have full cups. However, if a fifth friend arrives, everyone might need to take a little less so everyone could partake.

Prorating is a way for us to help the greatest number of members when there is a limited number of shares. We only ever prorate needs when there aren’t enough shares available – so far, not very often. If prorating has to happen three months in a row, the membership votes on a share increase to prevent future prorating.
