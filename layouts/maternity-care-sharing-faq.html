Do I have to add a second member prior to conception in order to share my maternity need?

No. Although we consider the unborn child to be a part of the family, we cannot add someone to membership prior to their birth. We need a date of birth in order to add someone to membership.

Any post-partum Need your baby might have would be shareable, as long as you add him or her to the membership within 30 days of birth.
