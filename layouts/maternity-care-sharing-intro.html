One-Person memberships
must choose one of the following options prior to conception for a maternity need to be shared:

Option 1

Add a spouse or dependant to your membership prior to conception.

The new Two-Person membership can include both the mother and a second individual, whether a husband or child. The membership must have at least two members prior to conception to meet the requirement.

Option 2

Add a spouse or dependant to your membership prior to conception.

The new Two-Person membership can include both the mother and a second individual, whether a husband or child. The membership must have at least two members prior to conception to meet the requirement.

See guidelines: Section IX for more information on maternity sharing.
