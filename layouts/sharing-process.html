How Sharing Works

  • 1 A Need Begins

    When a member has a Need, they receive treatment from a provider of their choice, collects the bills, and sends them to Samaritan Ministries office to start the need process.

    Go to the doctor of your choice
    Collect bills
  • 2 The Need is Shared

    The Samaritan Ministries staff verifies that the need meets the Guidelines, then directs other members to send their shares to the member with the need.

    Send your bills to Samaritan
    Samaritan assigns Shares to members
  • 3 The Burden is Lifted

    The member in need receives shares from the designated members to help pay their medical bills, along with prayer and notes of encouragement.
