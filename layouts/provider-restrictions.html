No Network or PPO Restrictions

Members of Samaritan Ministries aren’t restricted to seeing certain doctors or staying within a network. This freedom gives you the opportunity to choose the doctor, hospital, and pharmacy that works best for your family whenever you need medical care.

Samaritan Ministries doesn’t dictate which doctor you see, what treatment is prescribed, or how it is administered. Members may adhere to traditional medicine, or they have the freedom to pursue alternative treatment methods as prescribed by a licensed medical provider.

Know Where Your Money Goes
As a member of Samaritan Ministries, you can be confident that no money goes towards unbiblical practices such as abortion. Members are also exempt from the requirements of the Affordable Care Act.
