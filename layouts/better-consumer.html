A Better Health Care Consumer

Members are self-pay patients and are billed directly by their provider. Discussing discounts on your medical bills can be beneficial for you and the rest of the membership, and members are encouraged to discuss discounts with their providers. Receiving discounts on medical bills reduces the cost for the entire membership, therefore, lightening the load that members help one another bear.
